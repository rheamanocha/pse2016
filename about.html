About

The Bay Gallery was founded in 1986 by Sarah Weitz with the vision of showcasing modern art installations by young artists in the Bay Area. Since its inception, the Gallery has been a community landmark and tourist attraction.

All art on display is available for purchase. If you are interested in showcasing your art, please visit our Contact page.
